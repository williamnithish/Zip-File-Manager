## Conflict Detection

Generate embedding for candidate.

Search pgvector.

SQL:

SELECT
id,
title,
content,
1 - (embedding <=> candidate_embedding) similarity
FROM knowledge_nodes
ORDER BY embedding <=> candidate_embedding
LIMIT 5

Classification:

> 0.95
> DUPLICATE

0.85 - 0.95
UPDATE

0.70 - 0.85
COEXIST

< 0.70
NEW

Return:

{
"similarity":0.94,
"conflict_type":"DUPLICATE",
"existing_node":{}
}
